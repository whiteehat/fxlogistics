Company profile

About FX Logistics

A logistics operator built around one route that matters most to our customers: getting goods out of the USA, UK and China and into Nigeria, cleared and delivered without surprises.

What we handle

We manage the pieces that usually get lost between shippers, carriers and customs agents: booking freight, consolidating cargo, preparing clearance documentation, and coordinating delivery once goods arrive in Nigeria. One point of contact for the whole journey.

Where we operate

Our head office sits on Sheraton Link Road in Ikeja, Lagos, with additional offices in Ibadan and Ogbomoso to support customers across South-West Nigeria.
